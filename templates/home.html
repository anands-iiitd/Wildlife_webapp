CaTRAT: An AI-based tool for species segregation

CaTRAT is an Artificial Intelligence-based species segregation tool that leverages a deep learning-based object detector. Specifically, the YOLO-v5 object detector model is trained using over 1 million camera trap images acquired from all over India. The model can detect the presence of an animal, human or a vehicle and is capable of classifying the detected animal into one of 95 known species. The training data was curated with the help of MegaDetector for automated bounding boxes annotations and species labels manually annotated by experts.
